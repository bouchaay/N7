Router With Cache

2022

Ada Programming Language

I designed and implemented a router with a cache in Ada, forwarding data packets and storing frequently accessed data for improved system performance. The project required a deep understanding of network protocols, data structures, and object-oriented programming in Ada. Despite challenges with implementing the cache eviction policy and debugging routing algorithms, I overcame them through careful planning and testing. The final product demonstrated improved performance compared to a traditional router without a cache, and I gained valuable skills and knowledge for future computer networking projects.

A modem according to the V21 recommendation of the ITU

2022

Matlab

I implemented a V21 modem on MATLAB for binary data transmission over a frequency-modulated signal. The main challenge was constructing the FSK modulated signal, which required scaling and filtering. I also implemented a FSK demodulator with synchronization error handling using cosine functions. This project required expertise in digital signal processing, filter design, and MATLAB tools. I gained valuable skills and knowledge, which will benefit my future pursuits in telecommunications.

Climate Collage (Fresque du climat)

La Fresque du Climat is primarily a scientific workshop dedicated to climate change that stands out for its collaborative approach. This engaging and informative workshop facilitates a clear understanding of global warming and the various climate-related challenges we face today, similar to how the IPCC would present the information but with a more playful approach. Participants learn about the functioning of natural processes such as greenhouse gases (GHGs), the impact of human activities on the environment and ecosystems, the consequences of climate change on the socio-political and environmental balance of the world, and the solutions that can be implemented at both an individual and collective level. In addition to being an educational tool, La Fresque du Climat is also an association committed to raising awareness about environmental issues. It achieves this by organizing more and more Climate Frescoes and regularly training volunteer animators to lead them. The association now has over 6,000 volunteers, and its mission remains the same: to help people understand climate change and offer solutions for action.
